🌍 What is Environment?

Everything around us — living and non-living — that affects life on Earth.

🌱 Biotic (Living)

  • 🌲 Plants
  • 🐘 Animals
  • 👤 Humans
  • 🦠 Microorganisms

💎 Abiotic (Non-Living)

  • 💨 Air
  • 💧 Water
  • 🪨 Soil
  • ☀️ Sunlight
