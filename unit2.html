Natural Resources

🗂️ Classification Tree

Natural resources = materials from nature useful to humans.

🌍 Natural Resources
🌱 Biotic (Living)
🌲 Forests 🐾 Animals 🌿 Plants
💎 Abiotic (Non-Living)
💧 Water 💨 Air ⛏️ Minerals

⚡ Renewable vs Non-Renewable

♻️ Renewable Resources

Naturally replenished — never run out!

☀️ Solar💨 Wind💧 Hydro🌲 Forests

⚠️ Non-Renewable Resources

Limited quantity — will run out!

⛏️ Coal🛢️ Petroleum🔥 Natural Gas
🧠 Memory Trick
☀️ Sun = Renewable  |  ⛏️ Coal = Non-Renewable
