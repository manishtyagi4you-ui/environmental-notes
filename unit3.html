Ecosystems and Biodiversity

🔄 Energy Flow in an Ecosystem

☀️ Sun (Energy Source)
↓
🌿 Producers (Plants)
↓
🐰 Primary Consumers (Herbivores)
↓
🦊 Secondary Consumers (Carnivores)
↓
🦁 Apex Predators
↓
🍄 Decomposers (Fungi & Bacteria)
↓
♻️ Nutrients returned to Soil
🧠 Food Chain Example
🌾 Grass → 🐰 Rabbit → 🦊 Fox → 🍄 Decomposer

🌈 Types of Biodiversity

🧬
Genetic Diversity
Differences within a species
Example: Mango varieties
🐯
Species Diversity
Different species together
Example: Tiger, Deer, Elephant
🌏
Ecosystem Diversity
Different ecosystems
Example: Forest, Desert, Ocean

🛡️ Conservation Strategies

🏞️ In-Situ Conservation

Protection IN the natural habitat.

  • 🏕️ National Parks
  • 🦁 Wildlife Sanctuaries
  • 🌿 Biosphere Reserves

🏛️ Ex-Situ Conservation

Protection OUTSIDE the natural habitat.

  • 🦓 Zoos & Aquariums
  • 🌺 Botanical Gardens
  • 🌱 Seed Banks
