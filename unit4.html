Pollution and Health

🔍 Three Major Types of Pollution

💨
Air Pollution
  • Sources: Vehicles, Factories, Burning fuels
  • Pollutants: CO₂, SO₂, NOx, Smoke
  • Effects: Asthma, Lung disease, Global warming
💧
Water Pollution
  • Sources: Sewage, Industrial waste, Oil spills
  • Effects: Cholera, Typhoid, Aquatic death
🪨
Soil Pollution
  • Sources: Pesticides, Plastic, Chemicals
  • Effects: Reduced fertility, Toxic food
