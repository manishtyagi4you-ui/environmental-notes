Environmental Issues and Scales

📐 Environmental Scales – The Scale Pyramid

🌍 Planetary Scale — Whole Earth
🌏 Synoptic Scale — Country / Continent
🏙️ Meso Scale — City / Region
🏠 Micro Scale — Home / Farm

🔍 Local vs Global Issues

🏙️ Local Issues

  • 💧 Water shortage in cities
  • 🗑️ Garbage accumulation
  • 🚗 Urban air pollution
  • 🌊 Local river pollution

🌍 Global Issues

  • 🌡️ Climate change
  • ☁️ Ozone layer depletion
  • 🦋 Biodiversity loss
  • 🌊 Sea-level rise
