Climate Change

🏡 The Greenhouse Effect

☀️ Sunlight reaches Earth
↓
🌍 Earth Surface absorbs heat
↓
🔥 Heat released as infrared radiation
↓
☁️ Greenhouse gases TRAP the heat
↓
🌡️ Earth gets warmer (Greenhouse Effect)
↓
🌊 Climate Change & Global Warming

🏭
CO₂

🐄
Methane (CH₄)
Livestock, rice paddies
💧
Water Vapour
Most abundant GHG
🌀

  • 🌡️ Rising temperatures
